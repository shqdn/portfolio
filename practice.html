LE SSERAFIM Profile

Lesserafim Description

LE SSERAFIM (르세라핌) is a 5-member girl group under HYBE and Source Music. The members currently consist of Chaewon, Sakura, Yunjin, Kazuha, and Eunchae. LE SSERAFIM officially debuted on May 2, 2022 with their 1st mini album "FEARLESS". LE SSERAFIM had their first comeback on October 17, 2022 with "ANTIFRAGILE"

Members Positions**

  1. Chaewon, 22 - Leader, (Lead)Vocalist
  2. Sakura, 24 - (Sub)Vocalist, Center
  3. Yunjin, 21 - (Main)Vocalist
  4. Kazuha, 19- (Sub)Vocalist, (Lead)Rapper, (Main)Dancer
  5. Eunchae, 16 - (Sub)Vocalist, (Lead)Dancer, Maknae
